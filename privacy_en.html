Privacy Policy

'Local Restaurants Map' complies with personal information protection regulations under relevant laws, such as the Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization and Information Protection, etc., and the Personal Information Protection Act, and is committed to protecting users' personal information. The purpose of this privacy policy is to stipulate matters concerning the collection, use, and provision of personal information that occurs during the use of our services.

1. Personal Information Items Collected and Collection Method

This site collects the following minimal personal information to process partnership and general inquiries.

  • Items Collected: Name, Email Address
  • Collection Method: User's direct input via the inquiry form on the 'Contact Us' page

Additionally, during the use of the service, IP address, cookies, service usage records, device information, etc., may be automatically generated and collected.

2. Purpose of Collection and Use of Personal Information

The collected personal information is used for the following purposes:

  • Accurate responses to inquiries and securing a smooth communication channel.
  • Utilization for marketing and advertising, such as new service and event information (only with the user's separate consent).
  • Service improvement and statistical analysis.

3. Period of Retention and Use of Personal Information

In principle, users' personal information is destroyed without delay once the purpose of collection and use of personal information has been achieved. However, if retention is necessary according to relevant laws, personal information is stored for the period specified by those laws.

  • Records on contracts or subscription withdrawals: 5 years (Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce, etc.)
  • Records on consumer complaints or dispute resolution: 3 years (Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce, etc.)

4. Personal Information Destruction Procedure and Method

After the purpose is achieved, users' personal information is transferred to a separate database (or a separate document box for paper records), stored for a certain period according to relevant laws, and then destroyed. Personal information transferred to a separate database will not be used for any purpose other than those required by law.

  • Destruction Procedure: Personal information whose purpose has been achieved is stored for a certain period according to internal policies and other relevant laws, then destroyed.
  • Destruction Method: Personal information stored in electronic file format is deleted using technical methods that prevent the recovery of records, and personal information recorded on paper documents is shredded or incinerated.

5. Provision and Sharing of Personal Information

In principle, this site does not provide users' personal information to external parties. However, exceptions are made in the following cases:

  • If users have given prior consent.
  • If required by law, or if there is a request from an investigative agency according to legal procedures and methods for investigation purposes.

6. User's Rights and How to Exercise Them

Users can inquire about or modify their registered personal information at any time and can withdraw consent to the processing of personal information.

To inquire about, modify personal information, or withdraw consent, please go through the identity verification process via the 'Contact Us' page or contact the personal information protection officer by mail, phone, or email, and we will take action without delay.

Cookies are used to provide users with a better advertising experience and for website usage analysis, and do not contain personally identifiable information.
