About This Site

For those of you wondering what to have for dinner tonight, this site randomly recommends a variety of dinner menus with a simple click of a button.

Our goal is to solve the daily dilemma of "What should I eat today?" and provide a little inspiration to try new menus. We hope our recommendations bring a small joy to your dinner time.
